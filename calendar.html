CP3402/CP5637 Content Management Systems - Calendar (13-weeks)

CP3402 Calendar

Note: This calendar is for both internal and external students, with the only difference being that internal students have their prac in the week after the lecture (e.g. prac 1 in week 2), and external students will complete their prac in the same week (e.g. prac 1 in week 1).
The calendar below shows the practicals in the week row that is correct for external students.

The weekly schedule is planned so that you:

  1. Come to/watch lecture
  2. Watch Lynda,com learning videos (or otherwise do explicit learning)
  3. Come to/do practical
So, for example, the Lynda videos listed in week 5 are to be watched after the week 5 lecture.

Here is a playlist of all the Lynda.com courses.
"Copy" this playlist to your own account.
The playlist view is the easiest for seeing your progress, so show this to your tutor each practical.

Resources can be found on the practicals page.
Lecture notes (PowerPoint files) can be found on LearnJCU.
